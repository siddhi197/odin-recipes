Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description:

A grilled cheese sandwich is a beloved comfort food made by sandwiching slices of cheese between two pieces of bread, typically buttered on the outside, and then cooking it on a griddle or in a skillet until the bread is golden brown and crispy and the cheese inside is melted and gooey. It's a simple yet delicious treat enjoyed by people of all ages, often served with a side of tomato soup for dipping. Everyone needs to know how to make a classic grilled cheese sandwich. Whether you're a beginner cook or an old pro, you'll come back to this top-rated grilled cheese recipe again and again.

Ingredients:

  • 4 slices white bread
  • 3 tablespoons butter, divided
  • 2 slices Cheddar cheese

Steps:

  1. Preheat a nonstick skillet over medium heat. Generously butter one side of a slice of bread. Place bread butter-side down in the hot skillet; add 1 slice of cheese. Butter a second slice of bread on one side and place butter-side up on top of cheese.
  2. Cook until lightly browned on one side; flip over and continue cooking until cheese is melted. Repeat with remaining 2 slices of bread, butter, and slice of cheese.
